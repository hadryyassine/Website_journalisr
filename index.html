I'm Ahlam Ghajjou

My name is Ahlam Ghajjou, and I am a passionate journalist under-training with a strong background in producing multimedia content and traditional news reports.

My work

About

I am a graduate of the Higher Institute of Information and Communication of Morocco, where I earned my degree with honors. During my time at the institute, I had the opportunity to complete internships with some of the biggest news Organizations in Morocco, including Medi1 TV, the National Radio of Morocco, and UCLG Africa.

One of my proudest achievements was winning first prize in the "Best Journalistic Production" competition organized by the Higher Institute of Information and Communication,and the Swiss Embassy in Morocco.

I was also fortunate to receive a scholarship to attend a course on Mobile journalism at the Media Training Center in Lausanne, in Switzerland. In addition, I was selected as a finalist in the first edition of the TANDEM Media Awards organized by EU neighbours in Tunisia thanks to a radio-report I co-produced.

I have always been fascinated by the power of the media to inform and engage audiences, and I have dedicated myself to mastering the skills needed to produce high-quality content that meets the highest journalistic standards. Welcome to my portfolio! Here you will find my various journalistic productions in Arabic, French, and English.
